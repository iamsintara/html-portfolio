Crescent City Series by Sarah J. Maas

The "Crescent City" series, starting with "House of Earth and Blood," is an urban fantasy series by Sarah J. Maas. It follows Bryce Quinlan, a half-human, half-fae woman, and her quest for justice after a brutal murder. Teaming up with Hunt Athalar, a fallen angel, they uncover a web of political intrigue and hidden secrets, all set in a richly crafted world of humans, angels, and magical creatures. The series is known for its engaging characters, world-building, and a blend of romance and fantasy elements.
